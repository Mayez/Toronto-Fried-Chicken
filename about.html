About Us

From humble beginnings to a franchise chain

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Created from the vision and passion of our founder, John Doe who was formerly making fried chicken at Popeyes, believed that he could make a better batter and recipe for chicken. He decides to open up the first TFC store. 

  • 2012-2017

    Gaining customers

    This is where the most progress was made as TFC started to sponsor and host a bunch of public events all around Toronto for many years such as Canadian National Exhibition, Fan Expo, TIFF. 

  • Jan 2018

    Phase One Expansion

    This is where TFC started to expand to other locations within the GTA. This allowed us to gain much more exposure and gain national attention

  • Nov 2018

    Phase Two Expansion

    This is where we expanded to multiple location across the GTA and are continuing to do so till this day.
